Hello!


I'm Sherry and I love to paint and draw, and all things related to visual arts. I recently graduated from the University of Calgary with a Bsc in Psychology and a minor in Visual Studies. I mainly paint with acrylic and oil, but I also work with ink and charcoal.
If you're interested in purchasing any of my art or want something custom, please get in touch with me!
